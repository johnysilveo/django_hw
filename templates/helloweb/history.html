Keats — City of First Landings

Founded during the early Hegemony expansion, Keats grew around Hyperion’s primary spaceport on the Equus continent. Named in honor of poet John Keats, the city blended frontier grit with high culture: landing pads beside amphitheaters; cargo mechs idling outside coffeehouses where pilgrims swapped Time Tombs rumors.

As the largest settlement on Hyperion, Keats hosted the Hegemony Consulate and the Governor-General’s offices, serving as a launch point for expeditions into the Sea of Grass and toward the enigmatic Time Tombs.
